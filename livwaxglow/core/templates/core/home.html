THE SWEETER OPTION

Why choose sugaring?

Sugaring uses a simple sugar-based paste and a specialized removal technique that many clients find gentle and effective.

01

Gentle on Skin

The sugar paste adheres primarily to the hair instead of pulling heavily on live skin.

02

Smoother Results

Hair is removed from the root, helping skin feel smoother for longer than shaving.

03

Simple Ingredients

Traditional sugar paste is made with straightforward ingredients such as sugar, water, and lemon.

04

Less Breakage

The removal technique follows the direction of hair growth, helping reduce breakage.

BEFORE YOUR APPOINTMENT

Frequently asked questions

New to waxing or sugaring? Here are a few things to know before your visit.

How long should my hair be before my appointment?

Hair should generally be around one-quarter inch long, or roughly the length of a grain of rice. Avoid shaving for approximately two weeks beforehand when possible.

What should I do before my appointment?

Arrive with clean, dry skin and avoid applying lotions, oils, or heavy products to the treatment area beforehand.

Does sugaring hurt?

Some discomfort is normal, especially during a first appointment, but many clients find sugaring gentler than traditional waxing.

How often should I schedule appointments?

Most clients return every three to six weeks depending on the service, individual hair growth, and desired results.
